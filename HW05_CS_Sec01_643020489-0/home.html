9 website mistakes that's kill your conversion

1. Slow loading time

47% of the web users expect a web page to load in 2 seconds or less and 40% of people abandon a website that takes more than 3 seconds to load.

 2. Not Having a Prominent Call to Action (CTA)

You have to make it clear to your visitors what you want them to do.
3. Using the weak or common images

Images are one of the important and eye-catching design elements on a website.

 4. Not Being Mobile Friendly

As now-a-days lots of people are using smartphones and tablets for browsing websites and if your website is not mobile friendly then they will find your website broken when they will try to visit.
5. Confusing Navigation

You website navigation play a major role to convert the visitors into leads, subscribers or customers.

 6. Not Updating The Site

When the visitors come to a site that appears dated, they immediately think that this company does not keep up with modern times. Or, even bad, they may also think this company is no longer in business.
7. Poor Use of Color

Colors play a crucial role in web design. They help to highlight the buttons and links, make your site visually-appealing

8. Using Too Many Plugins

using too many plugins can increase your website loading time.
9. Unreadable Texts

Text still plays a major role in compelling people to stay on your website.
